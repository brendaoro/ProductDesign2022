Funnel Analysis: Users unable to select their discount
Focusing on the specific step during the funnel where the converion rate drops I compiled a customer journey using Hotjar videos and user's feedback I came to the conclusion that dropdowns were frequently misused.
Final Designs (for now)!
A button redesign can clearly show the selected and the alternative option(s) at once, making it easier for users to select their passanger category discount.
Applying my thoughts and ideas
"Selecting an option from a dropdown list (especially on mobile) is a multi-step process: you have to tap on the dropdown to open the list of options, then scroll and scan through the items to select one, and then close the dropdown."
