Discovery & Analysis: Identifying UX improvements
After analyzing the current funnel process for users, I could focus on the specific step where the conversion rate drops. Using the insights I gathered, I compiled a customer journey using Hotjar videos and real user's feedback I came to the conclusion that dropdowns were frequently misused.
This specific drop-down was especially important because users were not clear if their student discount was successfully reflected on the purchase.
